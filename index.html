About Me?

I’m a Senior Software Engineer. I started coding at the age of 12 and started my career at Ticketfly after college. I was the first Junior Engineer that the company ever hired, and I became a Senior Engineer in less than 2 years. I helped create various applications in both iOS and multiple front-end projects. I also worked to improve onboarding experiences, interviewed and recruited candidates, and created style guides for the company. I’ve also worked at companies such as Eventbrite and Pandora. I’ve created several programs in the companies I’ve worked at including a mentorship group that paired senior and junior engineers in a structured way to advance both careers. I’m a published O’Reilly author and speaker.
